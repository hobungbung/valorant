A first-person shooter game developed by Riot Games. In Korea, it is called VALORANT by taking the first two letters of the game name , and in English-speaking countries, it is also abbreviated as VLRT or VAL . In January 2021, as a campaign to commemorate the launch of Valorant , a game web entertainment coach Valorant was presented on the official YouTube channel with professional gamers and famous YouTube influencers . It is the only game from Riot Games that is not included in the LoL universe . Thanks to the introduction of a skill system while maintaining the feel of a military FPS , it has a relatively low barrier to entry for a heavy and tactical FPS, and is very popular with people of all ages. It also hosts strategic and extensive international leagues and world competitions , and is one of the most popular games that is gaining popularity as a next-generation e-sports, attracting 1.5 million viewers in just two years since its launch.
