Clove

1. information

A Radiant tactician agent from Valorant with immortal powersHe has aggressive traits that are not typical of tacticians, such as gaining temporary health or reviving by killing enemies, and being able to support allies by spreading smoke in death

2. skill

C : Pick Me Up

When activated, it absorbs the life of enemies it damages or kills, granting movement speed and temporary health increases.

Q : Meddle

Equip a shard of the Immortal Essence. When fired, it throws a shard that explodes after a short delay, temporarily corrupting all targets inside.

E : Ruse

Equip a battlefield vision. When firing, designate the location where smoke will appear. When secondary attacking, after confirming the location, send smoke that blocks vision in the designated area. Clove can use this skill even after death.

X : Not Dead Yet

When activated after death, you will be revived. If you do not get a kill or assist within a certain amount of time after resurrection, you will die. Activating again will cancel early.
