Omen

1. information

A strategist in Valorant and one of the first 10 agents to appear, he primarily uses abilities related to shadows . He boasts high survivability and psychological warfare abilities using teleportation and paranoia .

2. skill

C : Shrouded Step

Equip the Dark Footprints skill and mark the range. When you fire, after a short mental concentration, you will teleport to the marked location.

Q : Paranoia

Equip the Blindness Orb. When fired, it throws forward, briefly reducing the visibility and hearing of all players it touches. This projectile can pass through walls.

E : Dark Cover

Equip the Shadow Orb and enter another dimension to aim the orb. Press the skill key to throw the Shadow Orb at the marked location, creating a shadow zone that blocks vision for a while. Hold down fire to move the marked location further away. Hold down secondary attack to move the marked location closer. Press reload to toggle the primary aiming point on and off.

X : From the Shadows

Equip the Tactical Map. When fired, you will begin teleporting to the selected location. During the teleportation, Omen appears as a shadow, and if the enemy destroys the shadow, the teleportation will be canceled. Pressing the equip button will cause Omen to cancel the teleportation.
